## ðŸ“¡ Wireless Frequencies (Very Important)

Wireless communication uses **radio frequencies**.

### Common Bands

|Frequency|Characteristics|
|---|---|
|**2.4 GHz**|Long range, slower, more interference|
|**5 GHz**|Faster, shorter range|
|**Cellular (4G/5G)**|Wide range, varies by tech|

### Easy Rule to Remember:

- **Lower frequency = longer range**
    
- **Higher frequency = faster speed**
    

Governments regulate frequencies to avoid chaos and interference.
